Updraft

Her second ability is called updraft and this boosts her up for a short time before she ultimately falls back down. This ability can be used to play off angles like on top of a box, or to just reach higher elevations on the map in general. An off angle is an angle that is not commonly held. A reason why a position on top of a box would not be oconsidered a common angle is because not many agents can reach the top of that box. This allows for players to potnetially get some free damage from the surprise or even a kill. This costs 100 creds and she can have maximum one per game.
